RECIPE: FRIED EGG RICE

Ingredients:

  • Cooking Oil
  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Salt and Soy Sauce
  • Spring Onion

Methods:

  1. Step 1, mix the egg with rice and salt
  2. Step 2, heat the pan and pour small amount of cooking oil
  3. Step 3, add soy sauce and spring onion
  4. Step 4, mix until rice separates
  5. Step 5, serve and enjoy
